Face2Voice

Face2Voice uses face images and text as inputs (actual voices are shown for reference only).
Our goal is not to synthesize an exact human voice, but to synthesize a voice with features that correlate with the input face image.
Put the face image you want to convert to voice and listen to the voice in the text you want.
